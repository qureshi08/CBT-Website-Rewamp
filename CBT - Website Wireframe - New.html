What changes vs. what stays.

Four key moves shape the redesign. The rest is extension and rewrite.

Move 01

Partners in success.

Not body shopping. Kill the equal-weight 3-persona PersonaCards. Rebuild the home around one primary CTA: Book a Discovery Call.

Move 02

Split Products.

ECL Calculator gets its own hero page with KPMG credibility. Visuals become the AppSource receiver.

Move 03

Expand nav.

From 5 flat items to 8 + dropdowns. Add Industries, Case Studies, About. CGAP keeps its name.

Move 04

Named trust.

Logos lead: P&G, Coca-Cola, PepsiCo, UNICEF, ADNOC. Partner badges: Microsoft, Snowflake, KPMG, with tiers.

Working assumptions.

Where the brief was silent, I've made the following assumptions. Flag any that need to change.

  • → Discovery-call CTA links to /contact?intent=discovery today, upgrades to an embedded calendar (Cal.com / Calendly) in phase 2.
  • → The careers path keeps /cgap as its canonical route and CGAP as its brand identity. /careers is a friendly alias (301 → /cgap). Existing tables (cgap_cohorts, cgap_alumni) stay.
  • → Admin portal is unchanged structurally; schema extensions (services, industries, product categories, partner tiers) are phase 4.
  • → Programme duration is 9 months per brief (code currently says 6) — confirm before launch.
  • → Industries are outcome storytelling wrappers around case studies, not separate service offerings.
  • → The "For Partners / Careers / Products" tri-block at the bottom of Home replaces PersonaCards — same spirit, demoted rank.
